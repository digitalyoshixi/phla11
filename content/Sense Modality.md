Knowledge gathered from the 5 senses.
I perceive/feel therefore I know.
This is the basis of [[Science]] ([[Empiricism]])
# Counterarguments
### Senses are fallible
We can be halicunated or depraved of accurate senses.
Our senses may not track the truth.
